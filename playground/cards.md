# SUBIT‑Lingua Playground — Training Cards

These cards are designed for rapid practice with SUBIT‑Lingua forms.  
Use them for drilling, memorization, intuition‑building, or quick structural checks.

Each card contains:
- the form  
- axis / bigram  
- structural meaning  
- a prompt for exploration  

---

# 1. Atomic Cards (CV)

## WHO (K-axis)
**KA** — WHO × 00  
Prompt: sense the beginning of a subject.

**KE** — WHO × 01  
Prompt: sense boundary or definition of a subject.

**KO** — WHO × 10  
Prompt: sense depth or source of a subject.

**KU** — WHO × 11  
Prompt: sense peak or intensity of a subject.

---

## WHERE (T-axis)
**TA** — WHERE × 00  
Prompt: empty space.

**TE** — WHERE × 01  
Prompt: structured space.

**TO** — WHERE × 10  
Prompt: deep or layered space.

**TU** — WHERE × 11  
Prompt: energetic or active space.

---

## WHEN (M-axis)
**MA** — WHEN × 00  
Prompt: beginning of time.

**ME** — WHEN × 01  
Prompt: rhythmic time.

**MO** — WHEN × 10  
Prompt: deep time.

**MU** — WHEN × 11  
Prompt: peak or explosive time.

---

# 2. Doubled Cards (CV‑CV)

Each doubled form shows **modulation inside one axis**.

Format:
```
Axis × (Bigram1 → Bigram2)
```

## WHO (K-axis)
**KA‑KA** — 00→00  
Prompt: stable identity.

**KA‑KE** — 00→01  
Prompt: emergence of form.

**KA‑KO** — 00→10  
Prompt: emergence of depth.

**KA‑KU** — 00→11  
Prompt: emergence of intensity.

Prompt: dissolution of form.

**KE‑KE** — 01→01  
Prompt: stable boundary.

**KE‑KO** — 01→10  
Prompt: form deepening.

**KE‑KU** — 01→11  
Prompt: form intensifying.

(…and so on for KO‑x and KU‑x)

# 3. Structured Cards (CV‑CV‑CV)

These are the **64 SUBIT cube states**.

Format:
```
WHO(Vx) – WHERE(Vy) – WHEN(Vz)
```

## Example Cards

**KA‑TA‑MA**  
00 / 00 / 00  
Prompt: pure emergence.

**KE‑TE‑ME**  
01 / 01 / 01  
Prompt: stable form across all axes.

**KO‑TO‑MO**  
10 / 10 / 10  
Prompt: deep coherence.

**KU‑TU‑MU**  
11 / 11 / 11  
Prompt: peak alignment.

---

## Mixed Cards

**KA‑TE‑MO**  
00 / 01 / 10  
Prompt: subject emerging into structure, then into depth.

**KE‑TO‑MU**  
01 / 10 / 11  
Prompt: form → depth → peak.

**KO‑TA‑ME**  
10 / 00 / 01  
Prompt: depth entering emptiness, then rhythm.

**KU‑TE‑MA**  
11 / 01 / 00  
Prompt: intensity entering structure, then beginning.

# 4. Compound Cards

Compounds show **left‑to‑right modulation**.

## Two‑Unit Cards

**KA‑TE‑MU TA‑ME**  
Prompt: active subject → structured space → peak time → spatial modulation.

**MA‑ME KA**  
Prompt: rhythmic time → subject grounding.

**TO‑MU ME‑MO**  
Prompt: energetic depth → rhythmic deepening.

---

## Three‑Unit Cards

**KA‑TA‑MA → TE‑ME → MU‑MU**  
Prompt: emergence → stabilization → peak.

**KE‑TO‑ME → MA‑MA → TA‑KU**  
Prompt: form → beginning → spatial intensity.
