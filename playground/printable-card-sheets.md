# SUBIT‑Lingua — Printable Card Sheets

These sheets are designed for printing and cutting.  
Each sheet contains 8 cards arranged in a clean, minimal grid.

Use them for:
- study  
- drills  
- workshops  
- tabletop exploration  
- teaching SUBIT‑Lingua  

All cards follow the canonical SUBIT structure.

---
