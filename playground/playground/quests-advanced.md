# SUBIT‑Lingua Playground — Advanced Quests

These quests are long‑form, multi‑step explorations designed to challenge your
structural intuition, deepen your understanding of SUBIT‑Lingua, and activate
emergent reasoning. Each quest unfolds through stages. Complete them in order
or jump between them freely.

There are no correct answers.  
Only coherence, structure, and discovery.

---

# QUEST 1 — The Fourfold Engine

Goal: Construct a complete 4‑phase engine using SUBIT‑Lingua forms.

### Stage 1 — Identify the Core
Choose one structured form that feels like:
- emergence  
- form  
- depth  
- energy  

### Stage 2 — Build the Engine
Arrange them into a 4‑unit compound:
```
Unit1 → Unit2 → Unit3 → Unit4
```

### Stage 3 — Analyze the Flow
For each transition:
- describe the modulation  
- identify the axis shift  
- identify the bigram shift  

### Stage 4 — Collapse the Engine
Compress the entire 4‑unit chain into a single structured form.

Interpret the collapse.

# QUEST 2 — The Axis Labyrinth

Goal: Explore the geometry of axis rotation.

### Stage 1 — Choose a Seed
Pick any structured form.

### Stage 2 — Rotate the Axes
Apply:
```
K → T → M → K
```
three times.

You now have a 3‑step cycle.

### Stage 3 — Interpret the Cycle
For each step:
- describe the shift in subject  
- describe the shift in space  
- describe the shift in time  

### Stage 4 — Find the Fixed Point
Determine whether any form in the cycle feels:
- invariant  
- resonant  
- self‑consistent  

Explain why.
